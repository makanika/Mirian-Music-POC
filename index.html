I'm Mirian, and This is My Story

I got into music because of the beauty that I express through my students. While I bring out the musical talent in them, each student teaches me something about myself.

I have the privilege of learning about music from my students each time, creating something new and learning something new about myself. It's a journey of shared discovery, and I'm honored to be a part of it.
